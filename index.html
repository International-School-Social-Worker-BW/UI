Introducing the Educell Social Worker App

Software to empower social workers.

This app provides a way for school administrators and social workers to create, read, update, and delete student data.

Educell lets you enter your information quickly, keep it stored securely, and shared easily with those who need to access it.

In short, we help you manage your data better. And with better management, more students can be helped. This is especially important when schools are so understaffed in today's world.

More about Educell.

We help schools and teachers in Africa to use low-cost technology and free instructional materials to improve outcomes in their classrooms.

Our mission is to provide every child on this earth with an opportunity to gain knowledge about the world, power to act in it, and desire to be self-reliant and compassionate.
